Dataset contains 850+ entities (Wine, Beer, Dish, Cheese).

Dual-layer scoring engine: Objective compatibility via 56 granular, property-weighted heuristic rules; Subjective personalization via high-dimensional taste-vector association (Collaborative ML). Heuristic and ML powered recipe analysis gives instant and explainable pairing recommendations.
